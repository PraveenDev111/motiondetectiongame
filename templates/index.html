How to Play

  1. Position your hand in front of the camera
  2. Say "Rock, Paper, Scissors, Shoot!" and make your gesture
  3. Click the "Capture Gesture" button
  4. See the results and try again!

Game Rules

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Same gesture is a tie
