How to Play

  1. Position your hand in front of the camera
  2. Make one of the gestures shown below
  3. Click the "Capture Gesture" button (or press Spacebar)
  4. See the results and try again!

Gesture Guide

Rock
Make a fist
Paper
Open palm, fingers together
Scissors
Two fingers extended in V shape
Lizard
Hand like a puppet mouth
Spock
Vulcan salute

Game Rules

  • Rock crushes Scissors & crushes Lizard
  • Paper covers Rock & disproves Spock
  • Scissors cuts Paper & decapitates Lizard
  • Lizard eats Paper & poisons Spock
  • Spock vaporizes Rock & smashes Scissors
